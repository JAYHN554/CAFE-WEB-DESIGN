Our Story

Small batch.
Big care.

Ember & Ground began with a single roaster in a borrowed garage, a dog-eared copy of William Ukers, and a belief that great coffee is grown, not manufactured.

We work directly with farm families across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra — paying above-market rates, visiting annually, and sharing cupping notes that flow both ways.

Our Origins

Every bean carries a story of altitude, rainfall, and the hands that picked it. Here are the regions that make our menu sing.

🇪🇹
Ethiopia
Yirgacheffe

Jasmine and bergamot up front, trailing into stone fruit. The benchmark for washed African coffees.

🇨🇴
Colombia
Huila

Caramel sweetness, red apple acidity, and a long smooth finish that makes it our most popular espresso base.

🇬🇹
Guatemala
Antigua

Volcanic soil, high altitude. Deep cocoa, brown sugar, and a pleasant smokiness that holds up beautifully in milk.

🇮🇩
Sumatra
Mandheling

Earthy, full-bodied, cedar and dark chocolate. The one that converts people who thought they didn't like coffee.
